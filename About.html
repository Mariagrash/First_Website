Why saving memories Important?

We hold onto past memories, all memories because they mean something to us. Whether good or bad, they're a part of us, and a part of the learning experience we carry with us. Of course there are going to be positive and negative effects, depending on the memory. We want to hold on to those that are positive and those that remind us of the people we love. The good times we've had, those times in our lives that brought us happiness. Memories trigger nastalgia of times that have passed.
